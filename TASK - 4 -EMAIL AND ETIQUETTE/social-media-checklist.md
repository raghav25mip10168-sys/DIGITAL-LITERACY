# Social Media Checklist for Responsible Use (College Students)
Do's

* Think before you post – ensure your content reflects positively on you.
* Maintain privacy settings and protect personal information.
* Share accurate and verified information only.
* Be respectful and professional in comments and messages.
* Build a positive digital footprint that supports your career goals.
* Credit original creators when sharing their content.

 Don'ts

* Do not post offensive, harmful, or inappropriate content.
* Avoid sharing confidential academic or personal information.
* Do not engage in online arguments or cyberbullying.
* Avoid oversharing personal details like location or contact information.
* Do not post content that could harm your professional reputation.
* Avoid spreading rumors or unverified news.
