Stay Safe Online – Prevention Checklist (For College Students in India)


 Stay Safe Online Checklist

1. Use strong and unique passwords for each account, and enable two-factor authentication (2FA) wherever possible.

2. Never share your OTP, UPI PIN, bank details, or passwords with anyone — banks and officials never ask for this information.

3. Do not scan unknown QR codes or click suspicious payment links, especially when someone claims you will “receive” money.

4. Before making any UPI payment, always verify the recipient’s name shown on the app and double-check the UPI ID.

5. Avoid using public Wi-Fi for financial transactions, online banking, or accessing sensitive accounts.

6. Keep your phone, laptop, and apps updated to the latest version to protect against security vulnerabilities.

7. Adjust privacy settings on social media platforms to limit who can view your posts, personal details, and contact information.

8. Be cautious of urgent messages about job offers, internships, lottery wins, or account suspension — verify from official sources before responding.

9. Regularly monitor your bank statements and transaction history to quickly detect unauthorized transactions.

10. Log out from shared devices and avoid saving passwords on public or college lab computers.

If You Become a Victim of Cybercrime

• Immediately call the Cyber Crime Helpline: **1930** (especially for financial fraud).

• Report the incident on the **National Cyber Crime Portal:** [https://cybercrime.gov.in](https://cybercrime.gov.in)

• Inform your bank or payment app immediately to block further transactions.
